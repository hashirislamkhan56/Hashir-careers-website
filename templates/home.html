About Hashir

I believe that this program will provide me with the foundation and skills that I needed to pursue a career in the field of computer engineering with focus on designing embedded systems powered by Artificial intelligence primarily in the areas of concurrency cores, CPUs and robotics. As a capstone project, I want to redesign the expensive traditional chips and microprocessors, which consume excessive power, have multi-tasking limitations across industries, are bulky, and are notorious for slowing down processing systems. The goal is to achieve minimalistic system design with smaller chipset size and increased workload and power efficiency. Thus, new generation of chips and microprocessors can be used across variety of applications in multiple industries in its native form, eliminating the need to redesign for specific applications, saving cost and other resources to the corporations.
